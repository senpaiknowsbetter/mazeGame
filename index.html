How to play

Move through the mazes collecting as much gold as you can along the way before the time runs out.

Controls

W/Up Arrow - Move up

D/Right Arrow - Move right

S/Down Arrow - Move down

A/Left Arrow - Move left

Currency

Maze Completion: 500 Gold

Gold: 200 Gold

Penalties

Collect all the gold inside the maze before completing it to get all gold.
If not, half the total golds value is deducted from the maze completion gold.

Time

You will have 60 seconds at the begining of the game. Randomly located time cells can be found throughout the mazes which add an additional 20 seconds to your time if collected.

Objects

Red Triangle - Your player position

Green Circle - Endpoint to move onto the next maze

Golden Circle - Scores points

Blue Circle - Adds aditional time
